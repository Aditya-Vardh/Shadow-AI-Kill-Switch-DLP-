About
Shadow AI Kill-Switch — Data Loss Prevention for AI Interactions
🛡️ Privacy First: All detection runs entirely on your device. No prompt text or sensitive data is ever transmitted to any server for scanning purposes.

This extension intercepts prompts before they are sent to AI services, automatically removing sensitive data patterns such as API keys, PII, financial data, credentials, and custom org-specific keywords defined by your IT team.
